Barn Intro

The Barn is very spacious with it's 150sq.m (1650sq ft) surface. The property is surrounded by a 10 acre park with mature trees for shade and private drive way for absolute privacy.

There is a large 60sq.m covered terrace that gives on to a private park and the woods, through a 2.4m large sliding glass doors. On the other side of the 80sq.m living area there is another glass sliding door that gives on to the Jacuzzi and Pool.

There is a wonderful and spacious master bedroom on the first floor, all on it’s own. It’s composed of the bedroom, bathroom with a 1m80x80 bath, a study and a very comfortable dressing wardrobe.

There is all you need to feel at home here. On site you will find all you need to feel at home, from the fully equipped kitchen the Barbie ready for those warm summer evenings and a great TV area with a 50” Plamsa HDTV.
