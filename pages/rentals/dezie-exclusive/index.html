Exclusivity Intro

The 18th century property composed of a Cottage (150 sq.m.) and the Adjacent tobacco barn (150 sq.m.) has been renovated by the owners following the best of the French tradition. Each house sleeps 6 (12 people in total) and has en-suite facilities and large living areas and are surrounded by a 10 acre park with mature trees for shade for absolute privacy.

The pool and Jacuzzi have a large wooden deck with view on the wild nature. Children can enjoy pony rides in the peaceful countryside surrounding the property (free pony on site). We are situated to the north of the Perigord Noir, in the heart of the Dordogne and Vezere valleys.

This is a secluded rural location situated 8km from Vergt, 15km from Le Bugue with their numerous tourist attractions, weekly markets, night markets and selection of supermarkets. Grant and Christelle, the English owners live discreetly on site.
