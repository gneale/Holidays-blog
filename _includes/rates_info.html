Notes

Rate Reference

Rates are in Pounds, payments are made in euros based on the sterling exchange. The reservation is 20% and the balance is 8 weeks before your stay. Payments

We accept Paypal, wire tansfer and checks in euros. Credit card and online booking (Paypal) charge 2.5%.

Cleaning:

Cleaning for the Cottage or the Barn is €90(cash), and for the Villa is €70(cash). There is a security deposit for all the properties of £400(cheque). Both cleaning and security deposit should be given on arrival. The cheque is destroyed after your departure. Linen and towels (2 for Bathroom and 1 for Pool) are included. They are changed every Saturday.

Arrival and departure:

Arrival day is Saturday. The arrival is between 4 and 5pm. The departure is 10am.

Exclusivity for Cottage or Barn:

Exclusivity is assured if you are renting the Cottage and Barn. There will be no other guests on site in this case.
If you would like to rent the Cottage or the Barn and be the only guests on site please ask for this explicitly.

Exclusivity for the Villa:

The Villa is only rented with exclusivity. This is a private property.
